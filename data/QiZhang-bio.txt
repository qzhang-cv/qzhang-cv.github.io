{Dr. Qi Zhang} is currently a Researcher with the Visual Computing Center of Tencent AI Lab. He received Ph.D. degree from the School of Computer Science of Northwestern Polytechnical University in 2021. He was a visiting student at the Australian National University (ANU) between 2019 to 2020. His interests focus on camera calibration, image rectification, view synthesis, 3D reconstruction, as well as applications of light field imaging.
